Instagram Stats: How It Works

This tool helps you analyze your Instagram followers and following lists locally in your browser, keeping your data private and secure.

Step 1: Prepare Your Instagram Data

Export your data from Instagram:

  • following.json: Your list of accounts you follow.
  • followers_*.json: One or more files containing your followers.

Step 2: Upload Files

Select the following.json file and all your followers_*.json files, then click Analyze.

Step 3: Analyze Data

  • The tool will compare your following and followers lists.
  • It will generate insights such as:
    • Accounts you follow that don't follow you back.
    • Accounts that follow you but you don't follow back.
    • Mutual followers.

Step 4: Download Results

You can download the results for easy reference:

  • not_following_back.txt – Users who don't follow you back.
  • not_followed_by_you.txt – Users you don't follow back.
  • mutual_follow.txt – Users with mutual following.

Step 5: Tips & Safety

  • All data is processed locally; nothing is uploaded to a server.
  • Ensure you upload the correct exported files from Instagram.
  • If any errors occur, check the file format and try again.

FAQ

Is my data safe?

Yes, everything happens locally in your browser. No data leaves your device.

What if I encounter problems?

Double-check that the files are in the correct format. If issues persist, contact the tool administrator.

About Local-First Tools

We create privacy-first tools that process your data locally, giving you insights and functionality without compromising personal information.
